How (to) Absurd

Here on the moon, absurd is a verb. And all your friends are doing it, too! To go ahead and absurd with moonstuff, choose a character that suits you in this moment, and start clicking the arrow buttons like a maniac.

Center My Screen Shuffle the Characters

Flower Child

Tell Flowerchild to stop chewing with her mouth open. And give her a pat on the back while you are at it.

Loud Shoe

Loud Shoe is the life of the party. Disco doctor extraordinaire. Pro tip - find a way to talk about wild moose.

Jelly Spider

This happy little zombie chews broom reeds. Feed it avocados and waft it to make it happy.

Dreaming Squirrel

Dreaming squirrel recites Simpsons episodes with its beauty mask on.
